# Recru AI Brand Voice

## Company Name
Recru AI

## What Recru AI Does
Recru AI helps architecture, design, and creative firms hire faster using a hybrid recruitment model.

The service combines:

- Human recruiter expertise
- AI-assisted sourcing
- Candidate screening
- Outreach automation
- Affordable placement support

## Brand Personality
Recru AI should sound:

- Clear
- Practical
- Smart
- Human
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Direct

## Tone
Use simple business language.

Avoid:

- Overly technical AI language
- Corporate buzzwords
- Overpromising
- Robotic writing
- Generic AI phrases

## Example Brand Message
Hiring for architecture and design roles should not take months or cost 25% of a salary.

Recru AI combines recruiter expertise with AI-supported workflows to help firms find better candidates faster and at a more affordable fee.

## Key Words

- Hybrid recruitment
- AI-assisted hiring
- Architecture recruitment
- Design talent
- Faster sourcing
- Better shortlists
- Affordable recruitment
- Human-led, AI-supported
- Specialist recruitment

## Writing Rules

- Keep sentences short
- Explain AI in simple terms
- Focus on business value
- Speak to founders, studio owners, and hiring managers
- Show how Recru AI saves time and cost
- Keep the writing practical and specific
