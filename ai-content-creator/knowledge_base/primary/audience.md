# Recru AI Target Audience

## Main Audience
Recru AI serves architecture, interior design, real estate, hospitality design, and creative studios.

## Ideal Customers
The ideal customers are:

- Architecture firm founders
- Design studio owners
- Interior design directors
- Operations managers
- HR managers at creative firms
- Hiring managers at growing studios

## Company Size
Best-fit companies usually have:

- 10 to 200 employees
- Project-based hiring needs
- Limited internal recruitment support
- Urgent hiring needs for designers, architects, BIM specialists, or project managers

## Customer Problems
These customers often struggle with:

- Finding qualified candidates
- Paying expensive recruitment fees
- Spending too much time screening CVs
- Losing candidates to competitors
- Hiring urgently when projects start
- Managing inconsistent staffing needs

## What They Want
They want:

- Faster candidate shortlists
- Better-quality candidates
- Lower hiring costs
- Less admin work
- A recruiter who understands architecture and design
- A simple hiring process

## Emotional Pain Points
They feel:

- Overwhelmed by hiring
- Frustrated with bad CVs
- Pressured by project deadlines
- Worried about hiring the wrong person
- Tired of expensive agency fees
