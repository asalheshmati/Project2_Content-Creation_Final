# Recru AI Services

## Core Service
Recru AI offers hybrid recruitment for architecture, design, and creative firms.

The service combines human recruitment experience with AI-supported workflows.

## Main Services

### 1. AI-Assisted Candidate Sourcing
Recru AI searches for candidates using LinkedIn, job platforms, talent databases, and AI-supported search workflows.

### 2. Candidate Screening
Recru AI reviews candidate profiles based on:

- Role requirements
- Design experience
- Software skills
- Location
- Salary expectations
- Portfolio quality
- Availability

### 3. Shortlist Delivery
Clients receive a focused shortlist of suitable candidates instead of hundreds of unfiltered CVs.

### 4. Outreach Campaigns
Recru AI helps contact passive candidates with tailored messages.

### 5. Recruitment Process Support
Recru AI can support:

- Job description writing
- Candidate outreach
- Interview coordination
- Candidate follow-up
- Offer-stage support

## Pricing Idea
Instead of charging 15% to 25% of annual salary, Recru AI can offer a lower fixed placement fee.

Example:

- €5,000 per successful placement
- Optional monthly sourcing subscription
- Optional premium white-glove recruitment support

## Main Value Proposition
Recru AI helps creative firms hire better talent faster, with less manual work and lower recruitment costs.
