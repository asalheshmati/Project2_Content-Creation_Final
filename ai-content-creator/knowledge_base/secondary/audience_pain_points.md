# Client Pain Points

## Pain Point 1: Hiring Takes Too Long
Architecture and design firms often need people quickly when a new project starts.

## Pain Point 2: Candidate Quality Is Low
Many applicants do not match the role requirements. Some lack the right software skills, project experience, or portfolio quality.

## Pain Point 3: Recruitment Fees Are Too High
Small and mid-sized firms may not want to pay 15% to 25% of annual salary for one hire.

## Pain Point 4: Founders Are Too Busy
Studio founders and directors often handle hiring themselves, which takes time away from clients and projects.

## Pain Point 5: Passive Candidates Are Hard To Reach
The best candidates may not apply to job ads. They need personal outreach.

## Pain Point 6: Design Hiring Requires Specialist Knowledge
Generic recruiters may not understand:

- Revit
- BIM
- Rhino
- AutoCAD
- Hospitality design
- High-end residential design
- Technical detailing
- Portfolio quality

## Pain Point 7: Hiring Mistakes Are Expensive
A bad hire can delay projects, hurt team morale, and cost the firm money.

## Recru AI Solution
Recru AI reduces these problems by combining specialist recruitment knowledge with AI-powered sourcing, screening, and outreach.
