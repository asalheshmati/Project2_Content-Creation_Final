# Industry News and Observations

## Observation 1
Creative firms are under pressure to deliver projects faster while keeping team sizes lean.

## Observation 2
Architecture and design roles often require a mix of creative, technical, and client-facing skills.

## Observation 3
Hiring managers need more than keyword matching. They need candidate context, portfolio review, and role-specific judgment.

## Observation 4
AI tools are becoming more common in business operations, but many firms still need simple and practical workflows.

## Observation 5
The strongest hiring workflow combines technology with human review.
