# Recruitment and AI Market Trends

## Trend 1: Companies Want Faster Hiring
Many firms need faster hiring because projects move quickly and teams need talent immediately.

## Trend 2: Traditional Recruitment Fees Are Expensive
Traditional agencies often charge a percentage of annual salary. This can be difficult for small and mid-sized firms.

## Trend 3: AI Can Reduce Manual Recruitment Work
AI can help with:

- Candidate search
- CV screening
- Outreach drafts
- Job description writing
- Candidate matching
- Follow-up reminders

## Trend 4: Human Judgment Still Matters
AI can support recruitment, but it should not fully replace human decision-making.

Creative industries still need human understanding of:

- Portfolio quality
- Design taste
- Culture fit
- Communication skills
- Project experience

## Trend 5: Niche Recruitment Is More Valuable Than Generic Recruitment
Architecture and design firms need recruiters who understand:

- Design roles
- Studio structures
- Software tools
- Portfolios
- Project types
- Client-facing skills

## Key Insight
The best opportunity for Recru AI is not to replace recruiters.

It is to make specialist recruitment faster, smarter, and more affordable.
