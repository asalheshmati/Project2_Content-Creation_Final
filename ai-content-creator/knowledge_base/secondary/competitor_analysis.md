# Competitor Analysis

## Traditional Recruitment Agencies
Traditional recruitment agencies help companies find candidates, but they can be expensive.

Common weaknesses:

- High placement fees
- Slow process
- Generic candidate searches
- Less transparency
- Limited AI automation

## Internal HR Teams
Internal HR teams manage hiring inside companies.

Common weaknesses:

- Too many roles to manage
- Limited time for sourcing
- May not understand niche architecture roles deeply
- Often rely on inbound applicants only

## Freelance Recruiters
Freelance recruiters can be flexible and affordable.

Common weaknesses:

- Limited capacity
- Manual workflows
- Inconsistent process
- Less scalable

## AI Hiring Tools
AI hiring tools can automate parts of recruitment.

Common weaknesses:

- Too generic
- May lack human judgment
- May not understand design portfolios
- Can feel impersonal

## Recru AI Positioning
Recru AI sits between a traditional recruiter and an AI hiring tool.

It offers:

- Human recruitment expertise
- AI-supported speed
- Niche architecture and design knowledge
- Lower-cost hiring support
- Practical workflows for small and mid-sized firms

## Differentiation
Recru AI is different because it focuses on creative and built-environment hiring, not generic corporate recruitment.
